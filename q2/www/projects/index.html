Isaac Newton

Sir Isaac Newton, was born on March 31, 1727 and lived till the age of 84 (March 20, 1726), was a mathematician, physicist, astronomer, theologian, and author. Newton has made seminal contributions to optics, and shares credit with Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz for developing infinitesimal calculus.

Florence Nightingale

Florence Nightingale, was born on May 12, 1820 and lived till the age of 90 (August 13, 1910), was an english social reformer and statistician, and the founder of modern nursing. She became a pioneer in the visual presentation of information and statisticals graphs.

Alan Turing

Alan Turing born on June 23, 1912 lived till the age of 41 (June 7, 1954), was highly influential in the development of theoretical computer science, providing a formalisation of the concepts of algorithim and computation with the Turing machine, which can be considerd a model of a general-purpose computer.

Ada Lovelace

Ada Lovelace born on December 10, 1815 lived till the age of 36 (November 27, 1852), was an English Mathematician and writer, chiefly known for her work on Charles Babbage's proposed mechanical general purpose computer, the analytical engine. She was the first to recognise that the machine had applications beyond pure calculation, and published the first algorithm intended to be carried out by such a machine.
